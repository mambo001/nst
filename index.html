About Biri

Essentials about the island
Language:

Waray

Most locals can understand and speak Tagalog and English.
Currency:

Philippine peso (PHP, â‚±). PHP100 is around USD2.03, EUR 1.74, SGD2.80

as of July 2020
Modes of payment:

Cash, SMART Padala, Globe GCash

There are no banks or ATMs on the island.
Connectivity:

LTE SMART, LTE Globe

Electricity Info:

220V, 60Hz. Socket Type A, mostly.

Electricity is now available 24/7 at some places but mostly available from 12 noon to 12 midnight only.
Best Time to Visit:

February to May

Samar region is along the usual typhoon path. July-November flights can be prone to inconveniences.
Fees/Fares
  • Tourism Fee: P50
  • Tour guide fee (for 3 person): P300
  • Environmental Fee: P30
  • Pumpboat to Biri Port: P50
  • Motorcycle from Biri Port to Tourism Center:P10
  • Motorcycle to Biri Jetty: P65
Fees may vary from this list. Last updated July 2020
